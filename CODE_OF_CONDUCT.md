# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want this project to be a respectful, technically rigorous place for contributors from different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Bring sources when making technical corrections.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the guide for readers and maintainers.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language
- Deliberate misrepresentation of technical sources
- Spamming, trolling, or derailing technical review

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, edits, or contributions that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues through the repository's issue tracker or another maintainer contact channel listed by the project.
