# Professional Connector Engineering

*A Complete Reference for Rugged, Industrial and Military Electromechanical Systems*

`MIL-DTL-38999` - `MIL-DTL-26482` - `MIL-DTL-24308` - `MIL-DTL-83513` - `M12` / `M8` - `D-Sub` - `Micro-D` - `Harting HAN` - `RF/Coax` - `Hybrid`

!!! note "Core Mental Model"
    A connector is a controlled interface between subsystems. It carries power, signals, and data; survives the environment; defines the service boundary; and becomes a configuration-controlled item in your released design baseline. The datasheet and the applicable standard always win - verify every part-number detail before release.
